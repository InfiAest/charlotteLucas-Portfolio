SG Photography

16.08.2021 - 08.10.2021

A local hobby photographer and friend, Simen Gjelsvik, wanted a simple portfolio website where the focus was his photographs. He sells prints of his work to friends and family and was thinking about having a live site in order to sell more of his work.

Brief

To design a basic logo for Simen and design and build a simple page where he can showcase his work if he wants to.

The aim was to practice working for a real-life client and to practice my basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

This is still just a practice site and has not been filled out with any content from the client aside the few photographs I recieved from him in the design phase.

Tools used -

Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Vanilla JavaScript.

My thoughts

I am happy with the way the site turned out. It is simple and easy to use which is exactly what Simen wanted. Something I might do in the future is to remove the contact form and keep it as a simple link to mail to.
