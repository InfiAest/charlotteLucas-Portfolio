Holidaze

04.04.2022 - 29.05.2022

This Holidaze site was the project exam at the end of the final year studying at Noroff. We had to use everything we had learnt through the year to design and build a hotel booking site for a holiday company in Bergen.

Brief

To create a hotel booking website with a visitor side where users can search accomodations and make enquiries, as well as the administration side where properties and hotels can be added and booking enquiries managed.

Create an API using Strapi to store the data for the establishments, enquiries and contact submissions.

Tools used -

Adobe XD, Next.JS, Strapi, Heroku, Bootstrap, SASS.

My thoughts

I am very happy with the outcome of this website. I feel that it looks clean and professional and that it is also responsive and easy to use. I am proud of myself for how far I have come with both designing and coding during my time studying and this website is a good representation of everything I have learnt.
